How we choose our picks

We don’t run a test kitchen or time boil speed ourselves. Instead, we dig into the long-term feedback from owners on forums like Reddit and specialty cooking groups, plus the findings of trusted reviewers who have used these cooktops for months. Our focus is on what actually matters after the honeymoon period: does it hold up to daily use, does it behave consistently with different pots, and how noisy does the cooling fan get? We also track common failure points, things like buttons that stop responding, sensors that misread pan size, and overheat shutdowns that happen too easily. By combining owner reports with expert insight, we build a clear picture of which models earn their reputation and which ones fade fast. No single review decides our picks; it’s the consensus from real-world use that counts.
